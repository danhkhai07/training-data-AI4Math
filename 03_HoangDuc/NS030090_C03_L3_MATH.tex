\section*{Problem}

\text{Discuss the number of solutions of the following system of equations in terms of $k$, and solve the system using Cramer's Rule (for the values of $k$ where a unique solution exists):}
$$
\begin{cases}
kx + (1 - k)y = 1 \\
(1 - k)x + ky = 3
\end{cases}
$$
