\section*{Problem}

\text{Anton speaks French and German; Geraldine speaks English, French, and Italian; James speaks English, Italian, and Spanish; Lauren speaks all the languages spoken by the others except French; and no one speaks any other languages.}

\text{Construct the matrix $\mathbf{A} = [a_{ij}]_{i=1,...,4}^{j=1,...,5}$ where the rows carry information about the four speakers and the columns carry information about the languages they speak.}
\text{Set $a_{ij} = 1$ if person $i$ speaks language $j$ and $a_{ij} = 0$ otherwise.}

\text{Identify the languages corresponding to the columns (e.g., Column 1 = French, etc.).}
\text{Then, explain the meaning of the matrices $\mathbf{A}\mathbf{A}^T$ and $\mathbf{A}^T\mathbf{A}$.}
